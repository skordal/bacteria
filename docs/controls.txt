Keyboard Controls for Bacteria
##############################

Available controls:

A
	- Shows the number of the bacteria's ancestor.

B
	- Adds a bacteria at a random location.

C
	- Toggles the display of the coordinates of the bacteria.

E
	- Toggles the display of bacteria energy.

F
	- Adds a food nugget at a random location.

G
	- Toggles the display of the bacteria's generation.

Q/Escape
	- Exits the application.

S
	- Hides or displays the statistics.
